About the Race

5 Mile Race

  • Date: December 31st, 2025
  • Distance: 5 mile
  • Time: 12 Noon
  • Location: Loughmacrory
  • Male Record: 24:38 - Mark McKinstry, North Belfast Harriers, 2018
  • Female Record: 26:07 - Ciara Mageean, Team NB MCR/City of Lisburn, 2021

Kids Fun Run

  • Date: December 31st, 2025
  • Distance: ~1.7 mile
  • Time: 11:45am

All children who complete the fun run will receive a medal and post-race refreshments.

Race Day Schedule

  • 9:00-11:00 AM: Registration
  • 11:45 AM: Kids-race Start
  • 12:00 Noon: Race Start
  • Post race: Top 3 Male and Female Presentations

All runners are invited to enjoy post-race refreshments in the community hall after finishing.

Charity Information

All proceeds from this event will go to supporting two local charities:

  • Friendly Care Group Termonmaguirc
  • Termonmaguirc Cancer Patients Comfort Fund
