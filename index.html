About the Race

Our annual charity 5K race is a community event that brings together runners of all levels to raise funds for local charities. The race takes place in City Park and features a scenic route through beautiful landscapes.

Event Details

  • Date: October 15, 2023
  • Time: 9:00 AM
  • Location: City Park
  • Distance: 5 Kilometers
  • Registration Fee: $25

Race Day Schedule

  • 7:30 AM: Registration Opens
  • 8:45 AM: Pre-race Briefing
  • 9:00 AM: Race Start
  • 10:30 AM: Awards Ceremony
  • 11:00 AM: Post-race Celebration

Charity Information

All proceeds from this event will go to supporting local community programs including:

  • Youth Sports Programs
  • Community Health Initiatives
  • Local Food Banks
  • Educational Scholarships
