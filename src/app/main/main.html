Lough 5 Virtual Run, 2020

We have decided not to stage the Lough 5 race this year but to go virtual.

The virtual event will help support our local charities in this unprecedented time and help us mark our 12th year on 31st Dec, 2020.

To take part register at the link above, jog or run the 5 miles wherever you wish and send your result to us at lough5@live.co.uk.
