Race Location

Our charity 5K race will take place at City Park, a beautiful location with ample parking and facilities for all participants and spectators.

Address

City Park
123 Park Avenue
Anytown, ST 12345

Directions

From the North: Take Highway 101 South to Exit 25. Turn right onto Park Avenue and continue for 2 miles. City Park will be on your left.

From the South: Take Highway 101 North to Exit 23. Turn left onto Main Street, then right onto Park Avenue. Continue for 1 mile. City Park will be on your right.

From the East/West: Take Interstate 80 to Highway 101 and follow the directions above.

Parking Information

Free parking is available in the main City Park parking lot, which can be accessed from Park Avenue. Additional parking is available at the Community Center (456 Center Street), a short 5-minute walk from the race start/finish area.

Please arrive early as parking spaces fill up quickly on race day. Carpooling is encouraged.

Public Transportation

City Bus routes 10, 15, and 22 all stop at City Park. The nearest bus stop is "Park Main Entrance" on Park Avenue.

The nearest train station is Anytown Central, located 1 mile from City Park. A shuttle service will run between the station and the park from 7:00 AM to 1:00 PM on race day.
