Race Course

Our 5K course is designed to be enjoyable for runners of all levels, featuring scenic views of City Park and the surrounding area.

Course Overview

The race begins at the City Park Main Pavilion and follows a loop through the park's most beautiful areas. The course is primarily on paved paths with some sections on well-maintained gravel trails. The terrain is mostly flat with a few gentle hills, making it suitable for runners of all abilities.

Course Description

Start to Mile 1: The race begins at the Main Pavilion and heads north along Lakeside Path. Runners will enjoy views of the lake on their right as they make their way toward the Rose Garden.

Mile 1 to Mile 2: After passing the Rose Garden, the course turns east onto Forest Trail, a well-maintained gravel path that winds through a wooded area. This section includes a gentle uphill climb to the highest point of the course.

Mile 2 to Mile 3: Runners will exit the wooded area and turn south onto Meadow Lane, a paved path that offers open views of the park's central meadow. This section is mostly downhill, allowing for a fast pace.

Mile 3 to Finish: The final stretch takes runners west along Fountain Way, past the historic fountain, and back to the Main Pavilion for the finish.

Aid Stations

Water stations will be located at the following points:

  • Mile 1 (Rose Garden)
  • Mile 2 (Forest Trail Exit)
  • Finish Line

Course Rules

  • Participants must stay on the marked course at all times.
  • Headphones are permitted but discouraged for safety reasons.
  • Strollers are allowed but must start at the back of the pack.
  • Dogs and other pets are not permitted on the course during the race.
  • Littering on the course is strictly prohibited.
